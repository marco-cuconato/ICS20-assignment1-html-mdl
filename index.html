Rediet Abebe

Rediet abebe was born in Ethiopian and attended Harvard university and got her BA in mathematics. She attended Cornell university in computer science. Abebe research after university was in the field of AI and algorithms. In 2019 she work on the National Insities of Health working group on Al the group was tasked with developing new Al tools. She also co-founded Black in AI, a network of 1,500 researchers working on AI.
